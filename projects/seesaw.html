Seesaw

Samsung Life Insurance
Motion-Tracking Wearable Device for Senior
2024

See-Saw is an AI wearable that empowers active seniors to master balance anywhere. By replacing cameras with smart sensors, it delivers instant haptic feedback for real-time posture correction and sustainable well-being.
